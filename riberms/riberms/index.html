What it handles

Six rules. Zero headaches.

Every feature below exists to prevent one painful moment — a student showing up to the wrong exam, or missing the right one.

01

Same-day clash detection

When enrolling a student, RIBERMS checks if they already have an exam that day. Same shift or not — two exams in one day is blocked. No exceptions.

02

Semester eligibility rule

A student in the 7th semester can only take referred/improvement/backlog exams from Semesters 1 through 6. RIBERMS enforces this on every enrollment.

03

Custom shift & exact times

Morning or afternoon is just a label. What matters is the real time window — set it to the minute. 09:00–11:00 or 14:30–16:30, your call.

04

Smart course codes

Course codes follow a meaningful format: CSE2101 = Department + Year + Semester + Serial. RIBERMS even auto-suggests the next serial when you add a new subject.

05

Role-based access

Admins and teachers manage routines, add students, and create subjects. Students view their personalized routine — clean and filterable. Each role sees only what they need.

06

Filtered student view

Students filter by course, semester, department, shift, type, or date. The table updates instantly. Print-ready for exam hall reference.

Workflow

From empty schedule to published routine, in four steps.

STEP 01

Sign in by ID

Every person logs in using their student ID or employee ID. No usernames to remember.

STEP 02

Create the schedule

Admins and teachers add departments, subjects, and exams — each with a custom date, shift, and time window.

STEP 03

Enroll students

Assign students one at a time. RIBERMS checks semester eligibility and clashes in real-time before allowing the enrollment.

STEP 04

Students check their routine

Every student sees their exams the moment they log in — filterable, responsive, printable.
